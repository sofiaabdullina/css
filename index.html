As Stakes Rise, State Supreme Courts Become Crucial Election Battlegrounds

Issues like abortion, gerrymandering and voting have been tossed into state justices’ laps. Politicians, ideological PACs and big money are following. New York Times

Major League Baseball has a diversity problem, experts say. This year’s World Series is proof.

Recent news of there being no US-born Black players competing in the World Series for the first time since 1950 underscores what advocates for racial equity in sports say is the MLB’s decades-long struggle with diversity. A May report by The Institute for Diversity and Ethics in Sport found that there is now a smaller percentage of Black MLB players than there’s been in three decades. CNN
